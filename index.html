Frequently Asked Questions

What is a VPN and why do I need one?

A VPN (Virtual Private Network) encrypts your internet connection and routes it through a server in a location of your choice. This protects your privacy by hiding your IP address, secures your data on public Wi-Fi, and allows you to access geo-restricted content from anywhere in the world.

Is TC Tech VPN legal to use?

Yes, using a VPN is completely legal in most countries. However, we strongly discourage any illegal activities while using our service. Please check your local laws regarding VPN usage as some countries restrict or regulate VPN services.

How many devices can I connect simultaneously?

Our Basic plan allows 5 simultaneous connections, Pro plan allows 10, and Family plan allows up to 20 devices to be connected at the same time. You can use TC Tech VPN on all your devices including smartphones, tablets, computers, and even routers.

Will using a VPN slow down my internet speed?

While all VPNs may cause some speed reduction due to encryption overhead, TC Tech VPN is optimized for minimal speed loss. Our global network of high-speed servers ensures you get the best possible performance. Most users report only a 5-10% speed reduction, which is often unnoticeable.

Do you keep logs of my online activity?

No, we have a strict no-logs policy. We do not track, store, or log any information about what you do online when connected to our VPN service. The only information we collect is necessary for account creation and payment processing.
